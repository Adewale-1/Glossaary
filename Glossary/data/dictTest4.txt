significance
a concept one desires to express, primarily through verbal communication

expression
a term with its explanation found in a reference list

phrase
a combination of symbols in a linguistic system, containing a minimum of one symbol

explanation
a series of phrases that provide understanding to an expression

reference list
a collection of complex or specific expressions, accompanied by their 
explanations, typically located towards a book's conclusion

communication system
an assortment of character sequences, each possessing significance

written work
a documented or printed piece of literature
